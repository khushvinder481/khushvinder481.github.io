Health Services - Aarogya

The thing that would cause extreme stress to any sensitive person is that if a sick person dwelling in the villages situated in the forested mountainous areas needs medicine or treatment which is not available in his village, then what will be his condition? Vanvasi Kalyan Ashram is tirelessly working in the tribal areas to make Aarogya services available to all by various means such as medical centres, small hospitals, mobile hospitals (mobile medical units), Arogya Rakshak Yojana etc. Medicines or treatment facilities are provided to millions of patients throughout the year. Many dedicated doctors from cities contribute their valuable time.

During rainy season, medical camps are organized in small villages despite adverse transportation conditions. Thousands of medical camps are set up throughout the year benefiting lakhs of forest dwellers. Health services help preserve traditions, faith, and culture while providing life-saving medical assistance.
