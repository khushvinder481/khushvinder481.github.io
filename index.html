About Our NGO

Vansawasi Kalyan Ashram is a social organization dedicated to the welfare and upliftment of tribal (Adivasi) communities across rural and remote regions. Our mission is to provide better **education, healthcare, livelihood training, women empowerment, environment conservation**, and preserve tribal cultural heritage.

Established with the vision of creating opportunities for tribal families, we work through community volunteers, local branches, and development programs to improve the quality of life while respecting indigenous identity.
