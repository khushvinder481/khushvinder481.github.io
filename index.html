About Us

Vansawasi Kalyan Ashram is a dedicated NGO working for the upliftment of tribal communities. Our focus is on education, health programs, youth development, environmental protection, and preserving indigenous culture.

What We Do

Education

Scholarships, hostels, coaching centers.

Health

Medical camps, nutrition support, rural health services.

Livelihood

Skill training, women empowerment programs.

Environment

Tree plantation & awareness campaigns.
